## Rosemount Street and its vicinity in 1910
**Created by Pouyan Shahidi<br>
[This interactive map]( https://pouyan-s.github.io/Rosemount_1910/#16/43.7027/-79.5168) ([https://pouyan-s.github.io/TPL_neigh]( https://pouyan-s.github.io/Rosemount_1910/#16/43.7027/-79.5168)) presents two georeferenced plates from Charles E. Goad’s Atlas of the City of Toronto and Suburbs (1910), depicting North Station Street — now known as Rosemount Street (Weston, York) — and its surrounding area. <br><br>
Each time you click on the historical maps, they will cycle through full visibility (100% opacity), partial transparency (50% opacity), and complete invisibility (0% opacity), in that order. <br><br>
Use the layer control (top right) to toggle data layers, and explore the distance measurement and address search tools (top left). <br><br>

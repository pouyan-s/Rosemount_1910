Rosemount Street and its vicinity in 1910
Created by Pouyan Shahidi

This interactive map presents two georeferenced plates from Charles E. Goad’s Atlas of the City of Toronto and Suburbs (1910), depicting North Station Street — now known as Rosemount Street (Weston, York) — and its surrounding area.

Use the layer control (top right) to toggle data layers, and explore the distance measurement and address search tools (top left).

Map Source
Goad, Charles E. Atlas of the City of Toronto and Suburbs: In Three Volumes, Founded on Registered Plans and Special Surveys Showing Plan Numbers, Lots & Buildings. Chas. E. Goad, 1910. Plates 79-80
